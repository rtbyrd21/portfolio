Description

Created as a mock advertising campaign for Herbal Essences, this campaign was designed around several goals:

  • Simplify the vast amount of product selection Herbal Essences carries.
  • Make the experience enaging and personalized for the customer.
  • Create a campaign that will run well across a variety of platforms: computers, tablets, iphones
  • Create a campaign that lends itself to the potential of viral marketing through facebook and twitter.
The solution was an interactive space, which generated a product output based on the proximity of the cursor to several variables.

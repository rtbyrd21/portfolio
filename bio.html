A bit about myself

Finding solutions to creative challenges. That is the common link to the work in my portfolio. Many see a clear boundry between development, design and art. While these are very different fields, they are each bound together by common principles. When executing design for an interface, the code of a CMS or the composition of a figurative study, a well thought out plan must be considered first. Each medium has it's own set of tools, whether those are actual physical brushes and tablets or javascript frameworks; knowledge of these tools can lead to a flexible and maliable use of their application. There isn't an instruction manual for any of these lines of work, only best practices and a wealth of possibilities.

At the end of the day I want to create work that I'm proud of. This mindset has lead me down the path of web development, which ties in very well with my design background. Being able to execute designs into web frameworks has been very liberating. The development world is evolving quickly and I'm excited to be part of it.

Do whatever you do intensely. The artist is the man who leaves the crowd and goes pioneering. With him there is an idea which is his life.

Introduction:

This form describes a research project. It has information to help you decide whether or not you wish to partici- pate. Research studies include only people who choose to take part—your participation is completely voluntary.

You are being asked to take part in this study because we want to include adults who are not students of the study administrators. Please read this paper carefully and ask questions about anything that you do not understand with the project sta before deciding to participate.

Who is doing this research study?

This research study is being carried out by Master of Design Graduate Students at the University of Cincinnati. There will be other people on the research team helping at dierent times during the study. What is the purpose of this research study? The purpose of this study is to assist designers who are making symbols to be used in health care settings. These symbols will help people understand medical concepts. We are trying to make sure that people from all cultures and degrees of medical literacy can interpret the meanings of the symbols being designed.

To ensure that these symbols are as eective as possible, we need to know which symbols are not understood due to lack of relevant medical knowledge. Understanding why a symbol is not understood can help designers create a symbol that is clearer. Once the symbols are completed, they will be oered to health care practitioners across the United States.

Who will be in this research study?

About 40 people will take part in this study. You may or may not be in this study. Those being asked to take part in this study are because we want to include adults with advanced medical knowledge such as that held by medical students and professionals, and those without advanced medical knowledge such as young adult students studying in a healthcare profession.

What will you be asked to do in this research study, and how long will it take?

If you agree to participate, you will be shown a series of symbols that have all been designed to communicate a particular topic. Then you will look at each symbol individually and asked what you think the symbol means and what you might do in response to the symbol. You don’t need to explain your answers. An incorrect or ‘I don’t know’ answer is just as valuable in this study as a correct answer. You will do this for a number of dierent symbols. The full test will take about 25 minutes or less. The research will take place at various locations.

There are no risks to you for participating in the study. We do not record any personal data about you, other than your level of medical education.

What will you get because of being in this research study?

You will not receive anything for your participation, other than knowing you have helped advance knowledge in design.

Our Story

In the summer of 2013 I stumbled upon a large half-ton letterpress in the corner of an old manufacturing warehouse. It had been abandoned by the previous tenant and sat unused for years. The press, covered in thick black ink and cobwebs made the trip back to Cincinnati where it was restored to it's original working order as it did when it first was used in 1897. With a background as an artist, graphic designer and illustrator, the letterpress provides the perfect medium to transfer my artistic background to a variety of cards and invitations. I pride myself in providing high quality, entirely customizable work.

What we do

Illumination letterpress & Design is a full-service custom stationary design and print studio. Short-run, handmade and custom are keywords that come to mind when describing what we do. At the heart of our operation is an antique 1897 Challenge-Gordon platen letterpress. The press creates a unique impression on sheets of thick, heavy recycled stock paper.
